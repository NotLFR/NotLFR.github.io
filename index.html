§ 01 a short introduction

I keep this page for the same reason people kept journals before phones got loud — to think slowly in public. Most of what shows up here is half-finished. Some of it never finishes. That's the point.

If you came looking for a portfolio, the work is further down. If you came looking for a manifesto, you'll probably find one of those somewhere too. If you came here by accident, hi.
